LET´S HAVE A BLACK CELEBRATION!

Musta Juhla, which translates as "Black Celebration", is organized by Tampere Underground and Slice of Life in Tampere. This joint effort promises a unique celebration of the gothic subculture, combining music, art and a dark atmosphere. Musta Juhla aims to provide a platform for enthusiasts of the gothic lifestyle with an eclectic lineup of musical performances and a communal gathering of like-minded people. As Tampere welcomes this new addition to its cultural landscape, Musta Juhla promotes a sense of unity among the Gothic community and an appreciation of the many aspects of the sub-culture.
