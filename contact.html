CONTACT US

WANT TO PLAY AT MUSTA JUHLA?

Whether you're an aspiring artist, a seasoned performer or a band ready to rock the stage, we're always looking for fresh talent to add to our line-up.

Do not hesitate to contact us with your portfolio, demo or any queries regarding performance opportunities. Our team is eager to hear from you and discuss how you can be a part of the electrifying atmosphere at Musta Juhla. Drop us a line at mustajuhla@gmail.com and let's make some magic happen together!

VENUE

Located in the heart of Pispala, Tampere, Vastavirta-Klubi is a beacon of alternative rock culture. Established through the joint efforts of Vastavirta ry and Tahdonvoima Osuuskunta, this vibrant venue pulsates with the energy of independent music and artistic expression. Under the management of Rokata Oy, it continues to serve as a meeting place for music lovers and creative minds alike.

Currently Musta Juhla finds its home within these walls, adding to the eclectic tapestry of experiences offered by Vastavirta-Klubi.
