Located in the heart of Pispala, Tampere, Vastavirta-Klubi is a beacon of alternative rock culture. Established through the joint efforts of Vastavirta ry and Tahdonvoima Osuuskunta, this vibrant venue pulsates with the energy of independent music and artistic expression. Under the management of Rokata Oy, it continues to serve as a meeting place for music lovers and creative minds alike.

Also Musta Juhla finds its home within these walls, adding to the eclectic tapestry of experiences offered by Vastavirta-Klubi.
